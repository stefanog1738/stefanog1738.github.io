Independent Service Project

My ISP project is about a fightning game, similar to Super Smash Brothers. In this game, there are different and original characters from different medias, such as Batman, and a Pizza Guy. There are even special moves for individual characters, and unique animations for the moves. I was primarily motivated to create a game like this because I usually enjoy video games that involve fighting other characters, so when my partner came up with the idea for this, I was immediately hooked. Overall, I enjoyed making this game as it took a lot of planning to create the characters and moves. I spent a large amount of time trying to learn how to use blender for creating the models and animation, as well as Unity to create the game itself. There are a lot of issues that I came across, such as finding out a way to send our progress to our teammates, and longer loading times.
