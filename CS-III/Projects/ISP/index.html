Stefano Tower Defense

A Tower Defense style video game that will consist of multiple levels, each with towers to choose from. Each tower will have unique abilities and upgrades that will make them stronger and defeat the boss, Stefano. Different strategies will have to be made for each level as there will be different antagonists. The game will also have a story and each level will add a piece of lore into it. After completion of the final boss and general storyline, additional evels will be unlocked that will add a hidden story.
